PROJECT OVERVIEW

Do It Yourself, Not By Yourself

Currently, DIYers have a large variety of sources for project inspiration, however, these methods often leave users with a lack of clarity on project requirements including step-by-step instructions, needed materials, and a community. This causes users to feel frustrated, unmotivated to start a project, and ready to abandon projects. We want to provide users the space to do it yourself--but not by yourself.

How might we provide DIYers with a user-generated-content forum for DIY project inspiration, discussion, instructions, and tracking in a social app?

Meet Autumn O'Neill

  • Create DIYS that look good and can show off to friends
Needs
  • Needs a creative outlet to decompress from her career
  • Instructions with clear and simple to follow formats
Motivations
  • Wants to inspire other creatives with her projects

Frustrations

STORYBOARD

Bringing Joy to Life

Autumn is tired of scouring the internet for research DIY projects.

In STUDIYO, Autumn saves a Christmas wreath DIY to her saved collection and sets a targeted start date for December.

Autumn is encouraged to see project information and steps in one central spot.

After getting stuck, she consults the comments section to seek help and see what other users did.

Autumn is thrilled when she completes the crochet DIY in the estimated time. She recieves one badge, as well as likes and comments.

Autumn is not only cruising through other DIY projects, but she has found her own corner of the DIY community.

UI STYLE TILE

Clean, Inviting, and Authentic.

A bold color palette of orange, blue, and lime was selected to create a vibrant and playful brand image. Orange was selected as a primary color because, in color psychology, orange is meant to convey a sense of enthusiasm and adventure. This color selection plays into the idea that the pets are on their grand adventure to their forever homes.
