PROJECT OVERVIEW

Do It Yourself, Not By Yourself

Currently, DIYers have a large variety of sources for project inspiration, however, these methods often leave users with a lack of clarity on project requirements including step-by-step instructions, needed materials, and a community. This causes users to feel frustrated, unmotivated to start a project, and ready to abandon projects. We want to provide users the space to do it yourself--but not by yourself.

How might we provide DIYers with a user-generated-content forum for DIY project inspiration, discussion, instructions, and tracking in a social app?
